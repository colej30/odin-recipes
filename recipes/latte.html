Description

An Americano is a simple yet elegant coffee drink made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water. It's known for its clean, straightforward flavor that showcases the characteristics of the espresso while being less intense than a straight shot.

Ingredients

  • **Espresso:** A freshly pulled shot of espresso.
  • **Hot Water:** The amount of water is typically 2-3 times the volume of the espresso shot.

Steps

  1. **Pull a shot of espresso.** Follow the steps for making a shot of espresso and pour it into a mug or cup.
  2. **Heat the water.** Heat the water to just below boiling, typically around 195-205°F (90-96°C).
  3. **Combine the espresso and water.** Pour the hot water over the freshly pulled espresso shot.
