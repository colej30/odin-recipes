Description

Espresso is a concentrated coffee beverage brewed by forcing hot water under high pressure through finely-ground coffee beans. This process, called "pulling a shot," results in a small, potent drink with a distinct flavor and texture.

Ingredients

  • Coffee Beans
  • Hot Water

Steps

  1. Grind the coffee.
  2. Dose the coffee.
  3. Pull the shot into a glass
